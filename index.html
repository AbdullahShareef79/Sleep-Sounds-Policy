Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Deep Sleep Sounds ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free ambient sound application developed for Android. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal data.

2. Local Data Storage

The app saves the following data locally on your device only, using Flutter's SharedPreferences:

  • Your sound volume preferences and active sound mix
  • Saved custom mixes you create
  • Which premium sound packs you have unlocked
  • Sleep timer and Pomodoro settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Deep Sleep Sounds displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device advertising ID (GAID / IDFA)
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time via Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

4. Internet Permission

The app requests the INTERNET permission solely to load advertisements via AdMob. No audio, user data, or usage analytics are transmitted over the internet.

5. Audio & Microphone

Deep Sleep Sounds does not access your microphone or record any audio. All sounds are bundled within the app and played back locally.

6. Foreground Service

The app runs a foreground media service to continue playing sounds when the screen is off or the app is in the background. This service does not collect any data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, COPPA compliance is inherent to our design.

8. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDK:

  • Google AdMob – Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
